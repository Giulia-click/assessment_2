Venice Carnival: History, Masks, and Magic

Every year, Venice transforms into a mystical world where history, art, and celebration converge. The Venice Carnival is an enchanting event that draws visitors from around the globe with its elaborate masks, vibrant costumes, and age-old traditions.

A Glimpse into History

Originating in the 12th century, the Carnival has evolved over centuries, retaining its mysterious charm. Its elaborate masks were once used to hide one's identity, allowing people to transcend social boundaries.
